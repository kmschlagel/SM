    Statement Resume
 

Through painting, I work at nurturing my creativity and have found it best expressed in abstract art. Always growing, meeting challenges and developing a deeper understanding of what is important, helps keep the work alive and fresh, ever evolving.

I strive to project energy and motion with spontaneity, while at the same time maintaining a sense of balance, along with offering quiet places to rest. Keeping the basic principals of design in the background and taking them out only when needed allows for a wonderful sense of freedom and imagination during the process. Working in acrylics is the most flexible medium for achieving varied outcomes and using more unconventional methods such as squeegees and broad knives.

I believe color is another powerful tool. How we experience color impacts our emotional responses and is a major component to our self-expression. I use a very simple palette, preferring to mix directly on the canvas, producing a richer and more vibrant effect. Layering one color over another is also a technique that helps achieve that goal.

I want to draw the viewer in — bringing them into a piece with their own sense of creativity and self-expression — achieving another valuable goal, which is often elusive and challenging.
