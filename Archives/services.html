Interior Design Consultation
Between painting and working in the field of interior design, I have more than 25 years of experience. My focus over the past few years has been to develop my art which has greatly enhanced my ability to offer creative design solutions for your home.

Original, Commissioned Art Work
I am happy to create a one-of-a-kind piece to fit a color scheme or a specific space.

 

Home Staging
It is important in the current market place for your home to show well in order to sell. Giving your home a simple makeover is the advantage of staging, helping to sell your home in a timely manner.
